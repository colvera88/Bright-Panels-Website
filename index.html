Why clean your solar panels?

✓
Dirt, dust, and ash can block sunlight from reaching the panel surface.
✓
Bird droppings are stubborn and can create shaded spots on panels.
✓
Hard water spots and residue can leave panels looking cloudy.
✓
Routine cleaning helps panels receive more sunlight and keeps your system looking maintained.
